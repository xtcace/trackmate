How it works

Everything you need to run your jobs

From the first quote to the final invoice — TrackMate keeps every job organised and every dollar visible.

📋
Job pipeline
See every job at a glance across five stages — Quoted, Accepted, In Progress, Completed and Invoiced. Drag and update with one tap.
💰
Real profit per job
Add your parts costs and labour as you go. TrackMate automatically calculates your real profit on every job so you always know what you're making.
🔧
Parts tracker
Log every part and material used on a job. See the total parts cost instantly so nothing gets forgotten and every cost is accounted for.
📊
Dashboard overview
Active jobs, total revenue, total profit and completed jobs all on one screen. Know exactly how your business is performing at any moment.
📱
Works on your phone
Update jobs on site from your phone. Add a part, change a status, check your profit — all from the job site without going back to the office.
🔗
Mate Suite connected
One login works across all Mate Suite tools. Use QuoteMate to generate the quote, TrackMate to manage the job. Same account, same ecosystem.

FAQ

Common questions

Is TrackMate free? +
Yes — the free plan lets you track up to 5 active jobs with full pipeline, parts tracking and profit calculation. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited jobs and PDF reports.
Does it work on my phone on site? +
Yes — TrackMate is built mobile-first. Update job status, add parts costs and check your profit from any phone on any job site.
Is it connected to QuoteMate? +
Yes — one Mate Suite login works across both tools. Use QuoteMate to generate professional PDF quotes, then TrackMate to manage the job once it's accepted.
How does profit calculation work? +
Enter your quoted amount and add parts costs as you go. TrackMate subtracts your parts and labour costs from the quoted amount to show your real profit per job automatically.
Can I cancel Pro anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime with no lock-in contracts. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
